Spiced Budget-Friendly Chickpea Curry

This Spiced Budget-Friendly Chickpea Curry is a simple, hearty dish that's easy on your wallet but big on flavor. A medley of aromatic spices meets nutrient-packed chickpeas, creating a satisfying and healthy meal

This recipe is ideal for a weeknight dinner, and the leftovers are just as delicious the next day. Serve it with some steamed rice or warm naan bread to complete the meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 large onion, finely diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 inch of fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons of curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon of turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon of chili powder (optional, for heat)
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish (optional)

Recipe instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion, garlic, and ginger, and saut√© until the onion is translucent and the garlic is fragrant.
  2. Add the curry powder, turmeric, cumin, and chili powder (if using) to the pot. Stir well to coat the onions and garlic in the spices.
  3. Add the diced tomatoes and chickpeas to the pot. Stir well to combine.
  4. Pour in the coconut milk and stir again. Lower the heat, cover the pot, and let the curry simmer for 15-20 minutes. This will allow the flavors to meld together.
  5. After simmering, taste the curry and add salt as needed.
  6. Serve your chickpea curry over rice or with naan bread, garnishing with fresh cilantro if desired. Enjoy your budget-friendly, flavorful meal!
