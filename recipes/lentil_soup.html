Hearty Budget-Friendly Lentil Soup

Our Hearty Budget-Friendly Lentil Soup is a staple for anyone seeking a nutritious, affordable meal that doesn't skimp on flavor. It's a versatile, plant-based dish that is rich in protein and fiber thanks to the lentils. The vegetables bring in additional nutrients, while the spices add a depth of flavor that makes this soup truly comforting.

It's perfect for batch cooking as it freezes well, ensuring you have a wholesome meal ready whenever you need it. Serve this soup with a slice of crusty bread for a complete, satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of dried lentils, rinsed
  • 1 large carrot, diced
  • 1 large celery stalk, diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried rosemary
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 4 cups of vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Recipe instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the diced onion, carrot, and celery. Sauté until the onions are translucent and the other vegetables are softened.
  2. Add the minced garlic to the pot and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add the lentils, diced tomatoes, thyme, rosemary, and bay leaf to the pot. Stir to combine.
  4. Pour in the vegetable broth. Increase the heat to bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce to a simmer. Cover the pot and let it simmer for about 30-40 minutes, or until the lentils are tender.
  5. Once the lentils are cooked, season the soup with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve your Hearty Budget-Friendly Lentil Soup hot, garnishing with fresh parsley if desired. Enjoy this comforting, nutritious dish!
